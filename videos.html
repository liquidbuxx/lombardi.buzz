music videos


Gavin reached out to me to help Drill out with a music video for their final album, Permanent, and I'm soooo glad I got to work with them on this project. LONG LIVE DRILL!



Hour, the instrumental project of my buddy, Mike Cormier commissioned me to create some music videos for their album Ease The Work. We had such a great time brainstorming each of the ideas we had, and I think we covered the gamut of styles and tones.






Francis asked me to help him make two music videos for his album Separation. He is one of my favorite artists, and getting to work with him is always fun. He came to me with the ideas, and tolerated my input, way more then he should have.



Carmen and I have known each other for a long time. When she asked me to shoot a music video for their new song, Leap Day, it was a no brainer. We had an absolute blast making this video, and it came out great.
