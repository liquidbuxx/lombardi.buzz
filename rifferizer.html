Buzz's Cocktails

Click a drink for it's recipe :)

In 2020, I started to hang out with some friends while watching the Bachelor for a Bachelor-Fantasy League. During these shows, my friend would make me Cocktails from a recipe book by the bar Death & Co. That Christmas, my partner got me the same book, and I was hooked. I love trying new drinks all the time, and sometimes, I use this tool to experiment and try something new. I made this recipe generator in JavaScript based on a similar tool I saw in this Google Doc using spreadsheet formulas to randomly assign ingredients. The recipes above are some original cocktails I came up with! If you try any out, please let me know :)
